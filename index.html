Examples

OneShot

Colorplane was adapted to iOS for OneShot, an app for highlighting screenshots of text and sharing them to Twitter. (oneshot.link)

Svbtle

Writers on Svbtle choose a color to represent their profile. Colorplane helps them find just the right color. (svbtle.com)

Green Room

Colorplane was created for Green Room, a private group chat tool we built to share with our friends. Instead of avatars, users and rooms are represented only by a color. (green.33cc77.com)
